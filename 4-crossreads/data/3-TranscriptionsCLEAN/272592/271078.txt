About 11' oclock we got a shock for the Germans opened a terrific mortar bombardment on our trenches & they gave us particular Hell you could count as many as 10 in the air at once & you can see them quite plainly for they leave a trail of sparks behind them & when they turn in the air they glow like red hot bulbs, the pace they travel at generates the heat, you can hear them coming
