<p>This morning learnt it was a French Dump that had been blown up but of course heard no details.</p>
<p>Nov 20/16<br/>The Division 'pulled out' about a week ago &amp; we put in 4 days at Ribemont &amp; came on yesterday to Vignacourt (10 miles from Amiens) where we are to put in the rest of our spell.</p>
<p>The weather has been very cold &amp; several frosts &amp; our first fall of snow It was only a taste of what we must expect but I am not looking forward</p>
