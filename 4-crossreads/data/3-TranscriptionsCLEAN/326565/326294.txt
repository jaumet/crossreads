 Wednesday, 26/1/16 Still very cold. Issued with sheep skin vests last night.  Large number of Australian troops still arriving here from Gallipoli.  Pontoon bridge placed across canal directly opposite our camp.  Kit Inspection & pay during morning, stretcher drill, afternoon.
