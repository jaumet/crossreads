<p>has the 1st 2nd 3rd &amp; 4th Mates on his right hand side and the Chief Engr, Purser, Snr &amp; Jnr Wireless operator, on his left.  One steward is employed to exclusively attend to the saloon, so we receive very good service.  There is a crew of 57 made up as follows Skipper 4 Mates Purser 2 W/Operators, 6 Engineers 6 Stewards 2 cooks , butcher baker, scullion 12 AB's &amp; and remainder stokers.  The salary list is very heavy and most of the positions are sinecures.
